How To Use
This is a smart cookbook designed to make cooking just a little less daunting and time-consuming of a daily task. It can be difficult to figure out what to make, keep track of ingredients you have in the kitchen, and easily find recipes that fit your needs. We are here to help with those struggles! Check out all our features!
Features of our smart cookbook include the Ingredients Pantry and Recipe Search. To find out more about these features continue reading below into the How to Use section.
Ingredients Pantry: add all the ingredients in your pantry! You can divide it up based on pantry items, refrigerated, and spices.
Recipe Search: search for whatever recipes you are craving! You can filter by ingredients you have available, types of food, and even if you are looking for something on the healthier side.
